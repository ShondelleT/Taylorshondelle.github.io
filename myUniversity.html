About BMCC

Located in the heart of New York City, BMCC embodies the bustling energy of Tribeca’s cultural hub, the dynamic spirit of Wall Street, and the enduring promise of the Statue of Liberty. Here, every student is not just a learner but a vital part of our diverse community. At BMCC, we live by the motto “Start Here. Go Anywhere.” to champion accessibility and excellence, providing affordable, high-quality education that prepares students for real-world success. Our campus thrives on innovation and supports every student’s journey of self-improvement, offering ample opportunities for those eager to excel.
